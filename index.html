The Monty Hall Problem

The Monty Hall Problem is a well known conundrum based on probability

A gameshow contestant is given a choice of 3 doors. Behind 1 is a car, and behind the other 2 are goats. When the contestant has chosen a door, the game show host will open one of the other doors which will always have a goat behind it.

The contestant is given the option to stick with their chosen door or switch to the remaining unopened door.

The aim of this challenge is to prove Vos Savant's findings that statistically, the best option is always to switch doors.

Have a go and see how often you win by sticking vs swapping!
